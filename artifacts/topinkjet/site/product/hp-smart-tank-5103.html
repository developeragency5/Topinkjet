Overview

Same dependable cartridge-free system as the 5101 with extra ink in the box. The Smart Tank 5103 is the value pick for households printing hundreds of pages a month who want to forget about cartridge runs and keep cost per page rock bottom.

Smart Tank flips the inkjet economics on its head. Instead of buying cartridges every few months, you fill the tanks once and print for what HP estimates is up to two years of typical home use before refilling. The result is dramatically lower cost-per-page than any cartridge-based printer in the same class.

Every order ships free via Standard Ground inside the United States, backed by our 30-day return window and the full HP manufacturer warranty. Need help choosing or have a question after delivery? Our US-based support team is one email away — see the Contact page.

Set Up in Three Steps

  1. 1

    Unbox & Power On

    Remove all the orange protective tape, plug in the power cord, and turn on the printer. Load a few sheets of letter paper into the input tray.

  2. 2

    Open the HP Smart App

    Download the free HP Smart app on iOS, Android, macOS, or Windows. The app will detect your new printer over Bluetooth Low Energy and walk you through Wi-Fi setup.

  3. 3

    Install Ink & Align

    Insert the included starter ink bottles into the tanks, print and scan the alignment page when prompted, and you're ready to print from any device on the network.

Connectivity & Mobile Printing

The HP Smart Tank 5103 All-in-One Printer connects with Wi-Fi (dual-band), USB 2.0, so it integrates seamlessly into any modern home or office network. Mobile printing is supported through HP Smart, AirPrint, Wi-Fi Direct, which means iPhone, iPad, Android, Chromebook, macOS, and Windows users can all print without installing custom drivers in most cases. Wi-Fi Direct lets phones connect to the printer without a router — useful in shared workspaces, classrooms, or anywhere guest devices need to print.
