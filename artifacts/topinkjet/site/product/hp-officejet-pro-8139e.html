Overview

The OfficeJet Pro 8139e is the high-volume sibling in the 8000 series. Designed for teams that print thousands of pages a month, it pairs a 250-sheet tray with a 35-page ADF, auto-duplex printing, and full mobile management. The HP+ smart printing system delivers extra security and convenient cloud features.

The HP OfficeJet Pro 8139e All-in-One Printer is engineered for the way real small offices and home offices actually work today — a mix of email, PDFs, contracts, scanning, and the occasional fax. Wireless setup over the HP Smart app takes about five minutes, and the printer is ready to share across every device on your network.

Every order ships free via Standard Ground inside the United States, with a 30-day return window and the full HP manufacturer warranty. Our team is US-based and reachable by email any day of the week — see the Contact page for details.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Print, scan, copy, mobile fax — replaces a separate printer, scanner, copier, and (where listed) fax.
  • Up to 20 ppm black / 10 ppm color — keep meetings on schedule and stop waiting on warmup beeps.
  • Up to 4800 x 1200 dpi color — sharp text, accurate color, and lab-quality photos when you need them.
  • Wi-Fi (dual-band), Bluetooth LE, USB 2.0 — connect to any modern home or office network in minutes.
  • 250 sheets input — fewer paper-tray reloads and fewer interruptions.
  • 35-sheet automatic document feeder — load a stack and walk away while it scans, copies, and (where supported) faxes hands-free.

Who It's For

The HP OfficeJet Pro 8139e All-in-One Printer is designed for growing home offices and small teams that print every day and want a true workhorse all-in-one — without the expense, heat, and footprint of a comparable color laser.

Common Use Cases

  • Daily contracts, invoices, reports, and proposals
  • Two-sided document scanning, copying, and (where supported) faxing
  • Mobile printing from laptops, tablets, and phones across the office
  • Wireless network sharing across multiple users without a print server

Set Up in Three Steps

  1. 1

    Unbox & Power On

    Remove all the orange protective tape, plug in the power cord, and turn on the printer. Load a few sheets of letter paper into the input tray.

  2. 2

    Open the HP Smart App

    Download the free HP Smart app on iOS, Android, macOS, or Windows. The app will detect your new printer over Bluetooth Low Energy and walk you through Wi-Fi setup.

  3. 3

    Install Ink & Align

    Insert the included starter cartridges, print and scan the alignment page when prompted, and you're ready to print from any device on the network.

Connectivity & Mobile Printing

The HP OfficeJet Pro 8139e All-in-One Printer connects with Wi-Fi (dual-band), Bluetooth LE, USB 2.0, so it integrates seamlessly into any modern home or office network. Mobile printing is supported through HP Smart, AirPrint, Wi-Fi Direct, which means iPhone, iPad, Android, Chromebook, macOS, and Windows users can all print without installing custom drivers in most cases. Wi-Fi Direct lets phones connect to the printer without a router — useful in shared workspaces, classrooms, or anywhere guest devices need to print.
